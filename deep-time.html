ü¶ï Burgess Shale in Context

The Burgess Shale creatures lived during the Middle Cambrian Period, specifically around 508 million years ago. In our 24-hour timeline, this was exactly 9:28 PM - right in the heart of the Cambrian Explosion!

üåü The Burgess Shale Connection

When you look at Burgess Shale fossils, you're seeing creatures that lived during one of the most remarkable 7 minutes in Earth's entire 24-hour history - from 21:21 to 21:28 PM. This brief window saw an explosion of biological creativity that has never been equaled.

These weren't just any creatures - they were the experimental prototypes for virtually every major form of animal life that would follow. In those 7 minutes of deep time, nature was testing out body plans that would define the next 500+ million years of animal evolution.
